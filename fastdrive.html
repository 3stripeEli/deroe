FASTDRIVE!

General Info:

FASTDRIVE! is the first installation on the FASTDRIVE series, now written by Mustafa Al-Zahawi. This issue takes place in California, Los Angeles. The main character in this book attempts to steal a significant amount of cars and leave in 60 seconds. This was shortened down so the story doesn't get spoiled.

History:

The history to this comic is pretty interesting. Mustafa Al-Zahawi remembered the series one day and ditched it. Got the idea of vanilla car chases and mishmashed it. Added the title and decided to release it .
