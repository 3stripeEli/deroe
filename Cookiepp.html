_> Cookie++

Cookie++ is a simple cookie clicker that runs on the command line and is written in c++. The controls are as simple as c+enter and that is basic level understanding of the game. Certain keystrokes help you get a higer or lower score according to which keystroke you use!


Cookie++2 is Out! Now with more features than ever! Introducing the TAG VALUE! With this so called Tag Value, you can do many things with it, such as trade cookies for it, combine the scores and much more! The H5 and H0 commands are removed. And if you think that's all Cookie++2 has to offer, it now has soundtrack!
Many other things are added now!
